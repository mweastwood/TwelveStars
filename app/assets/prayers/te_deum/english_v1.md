{O God, we praise Thee:|te_deum_1} {we acknowledge Thee to be the Lord.|te_deum_2}
{Everlasting Father, all the earth doth worship Thee.|te_deum_3}
{To Thee all the Angels, the Heavens and all the Powers,|te_deum_4}
{all the Cherubim and Seraphim, unceasingly cry out:|te_deum_5}
{Holy, Holy, Holy, Lord God of Hosts!|te_deum_6}
{Majestic is the glory of Thy name.|te_deum_7}
{The glorious choir of the Apostles,|te_deum_8}
{The admirable company of Prophets,|te_deum_9}
{The white-robed army of Martyrs praise Thee.|te_deum_10}
{Throughout the world the Holy Church proclaims Thee:|te_deum_11}
{The Father of infinite Majesty;|te_deum_12}
{Thy adorable, true, and only Son;|te_deum_13}
{And the Holy Spirit, the Comforter.|te_deum_14}
{O Christ, Thou art the King of Glory.|te_deum_15}
{Thou art the everlasting Son of the Father.|te_deum_16}
{When Thou tookest upon Thee to deliver man, Thou didst not disdain the Virgin's womb.|te_deum_17}
{Having overcome the sting of death, Thou didst open the Kingdom of Heaven to all believers.|te_deum_18}
{Thou sittest at the right hand of God, in the glory of the Father.|te_deum_19}
{We believe that Thou shalt come to be our Judge.|te_deum_20}
{We beseech Thee, therefore, help Thy servants, whom Thou hast redeemed with Thy precious blood.|te_deum_21}
{Make them to be numbered with Thy Saints in glory everlasting.|te_deum_22}
{O Lord, save Thy people, and bless Thine inheritance.|te_deum_23}
{Govern them, and exalt them forever.|te_deum_24}
{Day by day we magnify Thee;|te_deum_25}
{And we praise Thy name forever, yea, forever and ever.|te_deum_26}
{Vouchsafe, O Lord, this day, to keep us without sin.|te_deum_27}
{Have mercy on us, O Lord, have mercy on us.|te_deum_28}
{Let Thy mercy, O Lord, be upon us, as we have hoped in Thee.|te_deum_29}
{O Lord, in Thee have I hoped, let me not be confounded forever.|te_deum_30}
