{Lord, make me an instrument of your peace|st_francis_1}.
{Where there is hatred, let me bring love|st_francis_2}.
{Where there is offence, let me bring pardon|st_francis_3}.
{Where there is discord, let me bring union|st_francis_4}.
{Where there is error, let me bring truth|st_francis_5}.
{Where there is doubt, let me bring faith|st_francis_6}.
{Where there is despair, let me bring hope|st_francis_7}.
{Where there is darkness, let me bring your light|st_francis_8}.
{Where there is sadness, let me bring joy|st_francis_9}.

{O Lord, grant that I may not so much seek|st_francis_10}
{to be consoled as to console|st_francis_11},
{to be understood as to understand|st_francis_12},
{to be loved as to love|st_francis_13},
{for it is in giving that one receives|st_francis_14},
{it is in self-forgetting that one finds|st_francis_15},
{it is in forgiving that one is forgiven|st_francis_16},
{it is in dying that one awakens to eternal life|st_francis_17}.
