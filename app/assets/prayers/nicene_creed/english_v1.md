{I believe in one God, the Father almighty, maker of heaven and earth, of all things visible and invisible|nicene_creed_1}.

{I believe in one Lord Jesus Christ, the Only Begotten Son of God, born of the Father before all ages|nicene_creed_2}. {God from God, Light from Light, true God from true God, begotten, not made, consubstantial with the Father; through him all things were made|nicene_creed_3}. {For us men and for our salvation he came down from heaven, and by the Holy Spirit was incarnate of the Virgin Mary, and became man|nicene_creed_4}. {For our sake he was crucified under Pontius Pilate, he suffered death and was buried, and rose again on the third day in accordance with the Scriptures|nicene_creed_5}. {He ascended into heaven and is seated at the right hand of the Father|nicene_creed_6}. {He will come again in glory to judge the living and the dead and his kingdom will have no end|nicene_creed_7}.

{I believe in the Holy Spirit, the Lord, the giver of life, who proceeds from the Father and the Son|nicene_creed_8}, {who with the Father and the Son is adored and glorified, who has spoken through the prophets|nicene_creed_9}.

{I believe in one, holy, catholic and apostolic Church|nicene_creed_10}. {I confess one Baptism for the forgiveness of sins|nicene_creed_11} {and I look forward to the resurrection of the dead and the life of the world to come|nicene_creed_12}.
