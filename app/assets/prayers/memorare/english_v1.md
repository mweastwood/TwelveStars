{Remember, O most gracious Virgin Mary,|memorare_1}
{that never was it known that anyone who fled to thy protection,|memorare_2}
{implored thy help, or sought thy intercession|memorare_3}
{was left unaided.|memorare_4}

{Inspired by this confidence,|memorare_5}
{I fly unto thee, O Virgin of virgins, my Mother.|memorare_6}
{To thee do I come, before thee I stand, sinful and sorrowful.|memorare_7}

{O Mother of the Word Incarnate,|memorare_8}
{despise not my petitions, but in thy mercy hear and answer me.|memorare_9}
