{Come, Holy Spirit, Creator come,|veni_creator_spiritus_1}
{From your bright heavenly throne!|veni_creator_spiritus_2}
{Come, take possession of our souls,|veni_creator_spiritus_3}
{And make them all your own.|veni_creator_spiritus_4}

{You who are called the Paraclete,|veni_creator_spiritus_5}
{Best gift of God above,|veni_creator_spiritus_6}
{The living spring, the living fire,|veni_creator_spiritus_7}
{Sweet unction, and true love!|veni_creator_spiritus_8}

{You who are sevenfold in your grace,|veni_creator_spiritus_9}
{Finger of God's right hand,|veni_creator_spiritus_10}
{His promise, teaching little ones|veni_creator_spiritus_11}
{To speak and understand!|veni_creator_spiritus_12}

{O guide our minds with your blessed light,|veni_creator_spiritus_13}
{With love our hearts inflame,|veni_creator_spiritus_14}
{And with your strength which never decays|veni_creator_spiritus_15}
{Confirm our mortal frame.|veni_creator_spiritus_16}

{Far from us drive our hellish foe|veni_creator_spiritus_17}
{True peace unto us bring,|veni_creator_spiritus_18}
{And through all perils guide us safe|veni_creator_spiritus_19}
{Beneath your sacred wing.|veni_creator_spiritus_20}

{Through you may we the Father know,|veni_creator_spiritus_21}
{Through you the eternal Son|veni_creator_spiritus_22}
{And you the Spirit of them both|veni_creator_spiritus_23}
{Thrice-blessed three in one.|veni_creator_spiritus_24}

{All glory to the Father be,|veni_creator_spiritus_25}
{And to the risen Son;|veni_creator_spiritus_26}
{The same to you, O Paraclete,|veni_creator_spiritus_27}
{While endless ages run.|veni_creator_spiritus_28}
{Amen.|veni_creator_spiritus_29}
