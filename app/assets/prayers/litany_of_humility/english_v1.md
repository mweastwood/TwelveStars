{O Jesus! meek and humble of heart, Hear me.|litany_1}
{From the desire of being esteemed, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_2}
{From the desire of being loved, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_3}
{From the desire of being extolled, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_4}
{From the desire of being honored, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_5}
{From the desire of being praised, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_6}
{From the desire of being preferred to others, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_7}
{From the desire of being consulted, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_8}
{From the desire of being approved, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_9}
{From the fear of being humiliated, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_10}
{From the fear of being despised, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_11}
{From the fear of suffering rebukes, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_12}
{From the fear of being calumniated, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_13}
{From the fear of being forgotten, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_14}
{From the fear of being ridiculed, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_15}
{From the fear of being wronged, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_16}
{From the fear of being suspected, Deliver me, O Jesus.|litany_17}
{That others may be loved more than I, Jesus, grant me the grace to desire it.|litany_18}
{That others may be esteemed more than I, Jesus, grant me the grace to desire it.|litany_19}
{That, in the opinion of the world, others may increase and I may decrease, Jesus, grant me the grace to desire it.|litany_20}
{That others may be chosen and I set aside, Jesus, grant me the grace to desire it.|litany_21}
{That others may be praised and I go unnoticed, Jesus, grant me the grace to desire it.|litany_22}
{That others may be preferred to me in everything, Jesus, grant me the grace to desire it.|litany_23}
{That others may become holier than I, provided that I may become as holy as I should, Jesus, grant me the grace to desire it.|litany_24}
