Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

Welcome to TwelveStars. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, secure experience while using our mobile application (the "App").

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

TwelveStars is designed to be an offline-first devotional application. We value your privacy and aim to collect as little data as possible:

  • Local Storage (Offline-First): All your custom prayer settings, favorites, reading logs, and history are stored locally on your device using secure local databases. We do not upload or sync this content to any remote server or third-party cloud.
  • Diagnostic and Usage Data (Optional): To help us identify bugs, diagnose crashes, and improve the application performance, we may eventually collect anonymous diagnostic data and usage statistics. This data is aggregated and does not contain any personally identifiable information.

2. Third-Party Services

We may use Google Firebase services to help monitor app stability and improve performance. These services include:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to collect anonymous crash reports and error logs so we can quickly resolve application bugs.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used to understand anonymous usage trends (e.g., which sections of the app are visited most frequently) to guide future feature development.

Firebase processes this data in accordance with Google's privacy policies. You can learn more about Firebase security practices here: Firebase Privacy and Security Policies.

3. Children's Privacy

Our App is a general audience devotional tool and does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. All data remains strictly on your local device.

4. Security

We value your trust and strive to protect your data. Since your prayer history and app data are stored locally on your device, their security depends on your device's operating system security and access controls.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or services. Any updates will be posted on this page with the updated "Last Updated" date at the top.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us.
